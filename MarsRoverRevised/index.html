Exploring Mars with Curiosity and Perseverance

Curiosity and Perseverance are robotic rovers designed to investigate Mars’ geology, climate, and past habitability. This student project summarizes mission goals, launch and landing details, and major discoveries.

Featured Rovers

Curiosity

Mission: Investigate Gale Crater to assess past habitability and study Mars' climate and geology.

Major goals: search for evidence of past water, detect organic molecules.

Launch & landing timeline →

Perseverance

Mission: Seek signs of ancient life and collect rock samples for future return to Earth.

Major goals: sample caching, characterizing sample sites, technology demonstrations.
