Mission History

Launch and landing timelines for Curiosity and Perseverance, plus mission highlights.

Timeline

Curiosity

  • Launch: November 26, 2011 (Atlas V)
  • Landing: August 6, 2012 — Gale Crater (Sky Crane)
  • Objectives: Assess past habitability; study climate and geology.
  • Major discoveries: Evidence of ancient streambeds, detection of organic molecules and complex chemistry.

Perseverance

  • Launch: July 30, 2020 (Atlas V)
  • Landing: February 18, 2021 — Jezero Crater (Sky Crane)
  • Objectives: Search for signs of ancient life; collect samples for future return.
  • Major accomplishments: Successful sample caching, Ingenuity helicopter demonstration, characterization of habitable environments.

Notes
