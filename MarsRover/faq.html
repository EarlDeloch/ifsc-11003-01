FAQ

What are the primary capabilities of Curiosity and Perseverance?

Both rovers analyze Martian rocks and soil, study the climate and geology, and search for signs of past life. Perseverance can also collect samples for potential return to Earth.

What were the mission goals?

Curiosity focused on determining if Mars could support microbial life. Perseverance is searching for signs of ancient life and preparing samples for return.

How do these missions tie into a future manned mission?

The rovers provide vital information about Martian surface conditions, potential hazards, and resources that will inform future human missions to Mars.
