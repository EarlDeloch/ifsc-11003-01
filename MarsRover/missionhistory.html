Mission History

Curiosity Rover

  • Launch: November 26, 2011
  • Landing: August 6, 2012
  • Major Discoveries: Evidence of ancient water flows, complex organic molecules

Perseverance Rover

  • Launch: July 30, 2020
  • Landing: February 18, 2021
  • Major Discoveries: Sampling Martian rocks, signs of ancient microbial life
