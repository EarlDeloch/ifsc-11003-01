NASA Mars Rovers

Robotic Explorers on the Red Planet. Learn about Curiosity and Perseverance, their missions, and their discoveries.

Curiosity Rover

Curiosity’s mission is to explore Mars’ Gale Crater and determine if the environment could have supported microbial life.

Perseverance Rover

Perseverance seeks signs of ancient life and collects samples for future return to Earth.

Behind every rover is a team of engineers and scientists designing, building, and monitoring these robotic explorers.
